The Role of MDX in Physical AI Documentation

August 1, 2021 · 2 min read
Physical AI Documentation
Technical Writers for Physical AI

As Physical AI and humanoid robotics become increasingly complex, effective documentation is crucial. This post explores how MDX, a superset of Markdown that lets you embed JSX, can significantly enhance the quality and interactivity of Physical AI documentation.

MDX allows you to embed interactive React components directly into your Markdown content, making complex Physical AI concepts easier to understand and explore.
